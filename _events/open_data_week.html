NYC Open Data Week @ NYU

Hosted by BUGS Open Source Club

The goal of this event is to engage the different campuses, schools and departments of the NYU community in a discussion about the topics of NYC Open Data and open data in general. Many courses, research groups and other organizations use open data. We want to see what these different groups are doing and to open avenues for collaborations.

Event Details

We are part of the NYC Open Data Week celebration!

WHEN: March 5, 2018 at 7pm

WHERE: Hemmerdinger Hall, Silver Center of Arts & Science, 100 Washington Sq. East, New York City, NY 10012

REGISTER: Registration is free, but required (we need to know how many people will be attending to best plan for the event). If you already have the registration password, you can register here. Otherwise, complete this form and we will send you an invitation.
