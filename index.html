NYU's Open Source Club

Open source practices benefit education, advocacy, development, and involvement in the computer and technology communities. We apply these practices in the building of practice and creative projects and applications.

BUGS is focused on the promotion of open source practices through the engagement of short and long term projects, both practical and creative. These projects may be student, faculty, or outside expert led, with explanation of how open source

We are aimed at attracting all students who have a passion to learn and engage in projects within the computer and technology fields.
